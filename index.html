What is Open Source Show & Tell!

We love Open Source software and so do you. Come celebrate hacker culture on Friday, June 9th with Github, Google, Microsoft and Keen IO.

Open Source Show and Tell is an opportunity to share what you've been working on, learn about projects that your local community is supporting, and meet like minded people.
